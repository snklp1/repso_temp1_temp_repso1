1. Alphaget

We tell them an alphabet and they’ll have to bring stuff whose name starts with that letter. Things which have been given names by them won’t be considered (like a plant they call chintu for letter c). Only things which are named publicly.

Everyone gets the same letter or we decide with Math.rand() from a set of alphabets which have easier availibilty (i.e. removing z,q,x, etc).
The one who gets all the fastest shall win.

If draw, then tougher alphabets in a lower time limit.

2. Title-Monger

The final years will vote on titles we announce. One senior gets one title. Once they get a title, they’ll be out of the race to get another but still can vote. If in the case another title comes up which better suits an already title winner, the previously awarded title can be withdrawn and it will be up for grabs again.

Exemplar titles: The Expert, The Party monster, etc
